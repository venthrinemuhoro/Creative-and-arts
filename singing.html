Wedding Singers
 

Book a singer to bring that extra musical magic and create an event your guests will remember!

Whether you’re looking for up-front, stand-out vocals to wow the guests at a big celebration such as a wedding or birthday bash, or you’re in need of some ambient background music for a cocktail party, corporate event or after-dinner entertainment, hiring a singer can be the perfect choice of entertainment.

Want to find out more about singers for hire? Read our comprehensive guide about How to Hire a Singer. It covers everything you need to know from when to book, how much a singer is likely to cost, how long their set is likely to be and booking essentials.

Singers attend special occasions to perform live for entertainment at weddings, parties and corporate events.

They can be accompanied by great quality, professional backin g tracks, a live guitarist or keyboard player, or a full band.  

It’s possible to hire a singer to perform any genre of music, from rock ’n’ roll  to opera, and nothing is more certain to give your guests a thrill and bring a touch of magic to your event.
